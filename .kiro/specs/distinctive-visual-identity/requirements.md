# Requirements Document

## Introduction

This feature aims to transform the IFS Journal app from a generic, AI-generated appearance into a distinctive, memorable visual experience. The design will maintain clean, intuitive usability while introducing unique visual elements that reflect the therapeutic nature of Internal Family Systems workâ€”exploring internal parts, self-discovery, and emotional depth.

## Glossary

- **Visual_Identity_System**: The cohesive set of design elements including color palette, typography, spacing, and visual motifs that create a distinctive brand experience
- **IFS_App**: The Internal Family Systems journaling application
- **User_Interface**: The visual and interactive elements through which users interact with the IFS_App
- **Design_Token**: A reusable design value (color, spacing, typography) that ensures consistency across the User_Interface
- **Visual_Hierarchy**: The arrangement of design elements to guide user attention and understanding
- **Therapeutic_Aesthetic**: A visual style that conveys safety, depth, introspection, and emotional authenticity

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a user, I want the app to have a unique visual personality that feels therapeutic and introspective, so that the interface supports my emotional work rather than feeling like a generic productivity tool

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user views any page of the IFS_App, THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use a sepia-toned color palette that evokes introspection and emotional depth
2. WHEN a user navigates through the IFS_App, THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL maintain consistent visual language across all pages
3. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL incorporate organic, flowing visual elements that contrast with typical rigid, corporate design patterns
4. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use typography that balances readability with personality
5. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL create visual distinction without compromising usability or accessibility

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a user, I want the interface to feel calming and supportive, so that I feel safe exploring difficult emotions and internal parts

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use a sepia-toned color palette with warm, muted, sophisticated tones rather than bright, saturated colors
2. WHEN displaying interactive elements, THE User_Interface SHALL use subtle animations and transitions that feel gentle rather than jarring
3. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL incorporate generous whitespace to create breathing room and reduce visual overwhelm
4. THE User_Interface SHALL use soft, rounded corners and organic shapes rather than sharp, angular elements
5. WHEN displaying text content, THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use comfortable line heights and spacing that support extended reading

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a user, I want visual elements that reflect the concept of internal parts and self-discovery, so that the design reinforces the therapeutic framework

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL incorporate subtle visual metaphors related to internal exploration, multiplicity, or emotional depth
2. WHEN displaying parts-related content, THE User_Interface SHALL use distinctive visual treatments that differentiate part types (Protectors, Managers, Firefighters, Exiles)
3. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use layering, depth, or transparency effects to suggest internal complexity
4. THE User_Interface SHALL incorporate custom iconography that feels hand-crafted rather than generic
5. WHEN displaying navigation or section headers, THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use visual elements that create a sense of journey or progression

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a user, I want the design to feel modern and polished, so that I trust the app with my personal therapeutic work

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use contemporary design patterns that feel current without following fleeting trends
2. WHEN rendering UI components, THE User_Interface SHALL display crisp, high-quality visual elements with attention to detail
3. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use consistent spacing and alignment that demonstrates design intentionality
4. THE User_Interface SHALL incorporate micro-interactions that provide feedback and delight without distraction
5. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL balance uniqueness with professionalism to maintain credibility

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a user, I want the distinctive design to enhance rather than hinder my ability to use the app, so that aesthetics support functionality

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL maintain WCAG 2.1 AA contrast ratios for all text and interactive elements
2. WHEN a user interacts with any UI element, THE User_Interface SHALL provide clear visual feedback indicating the element's state
3. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL use Visual_Hierarchy to guide users toward primary actions and important content
4. THE User_Interface SHALL maintain fast load times and smooth performance despite enhanced visual elements
5. WHEN displaying forms or data entry fields, THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL ensure clarity and ease of use are not compromised by aesthetic choices

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a user, I want the app to work seamlessly on my mobile device, so that I can journal and explore my parts wherever I am

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user accesses the IFS_App on a mobile device, THE User_Interface SHALL adapt layouts to fit smaller screen sizes
2. THE User_Interface SHALL provide touch-friendly interactive elements with minimum 44x44 pixel touch targets on mobile devices
3. WHEN a user rotates their mobile device, THE User_Interface SHALL adjust the layout appropriately for portrait and landscape orientations
4. THE Visual_Identity_System SHALL maintain visual consistency and brand identity across all screen sizes from mobile to desktop
5. WHEN displaying navigation on mobile devices, THE User_Interface SHALL use space-efficient patterns such as collapsible menus or bottom navigation
